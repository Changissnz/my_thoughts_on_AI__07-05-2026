My thoughts on the "AI" industry, along with personal asides, as of this day, 7/5/2026.

Based on my years since the end of 2019, and the experience culminating up to this point in 
time, especially in the last year, I do not have favorable views of multiple American industries 
and institutions.

There's a stagnation occurring for many demographics in the nation. I'm not saying I'm an 
all-knowing person that is so empathetic to the individual plights of everyone in this country. 
I'm saying that is what I believe based on personal experience and the anecdotes I've read. I 
do not think these times are politically stable. I think the deterioration and dishonesty of 
social media companies that came to light about 12 years ago (yet somehow, they are still thriving), 
an industry that conveniently leads to artificial intelligence due to so much data the organizations 
collect on individuals, does not bode well for the emerging welfare under artificial intelligence. 
I will go a little in-depth into why I believe this, as to dispel the readers' belief that I am merely 
being a "negative nancy", a cynic. 

First off, there are rogue counter-intelligence and malicious intelligence campaigns that have 
obviously been occurring since a long time ago. However, these past two decades, especially, have 
seen these activities fueled by computerized efficiencies. So let's say that the premise, the objective, 
is not entirely for national security. Let's entertain that. Commercial objectives, which somewhere along 
the lines fall into the benefit or subversion of national security. Without having to write so many things 
about commercial objectives, the painfully obvious implication of this is that all is lost for most people. 
Somewhere through the process of commercializing information, after making use of the electrical grid, the 
money becomes the talking point of a few rare winners. And everyone else is left wondering, "WTF is the 
financial benefit from this?" That was what went on with social media after its golden age decade. It turned from 
a forum-centric culture into a platform for businesses and hyper-political messaging. By certain regards, it 
sure seemed that the social media platforms were used for purposes a little more militaristic and political 
than what those corporatist heads were going on about, as seen by how frequently retarded they act onstage at 
San Francisco conferences. I have never been to those conferences. I am merely speaking from the Youtube videos 
I've seen of them and through hearsay from their co-conspiring academicians... and of course, who could ever forget 
the military?

technology corporation and in effect, **in whispers** be a spy for us." Like wow...! Wow...! How flattering, the life of a 
commercial spy. What a great and special deal. 

The "K-shaped economy" was featured in the news during this past year. And then that gave way to the "G-shaped economy", 
a.k.a. predominantly government subsidized. So that really boggles my mind where a lot of these financial numbers come 
from. Are they really wages to work for community or society, or are they wages that are merely two or three steps lower
